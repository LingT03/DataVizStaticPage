Project 3

Analysis

Key takeaways from this project and exploration of the healthcare data are that there are significant cost variation by procedure which was to be expected however the underlying discovery was the financial burden of high-complexity of surgeries that most providers do an inadequate job of managing their expenses. Regional trends show the disproportionate demand of healthcare services in urban areas like New York and California that cry for re/allocation of resources. My Dashboard shows that healthcare costs vary by procedure, urban areas have higher patient demand, Medicare payments depend on procedure complexity, and costs differ significantly across regions.
